Meet our Founder

Surshree Ayyer is an architect, artist and researcher working on diverse architectural and interior design challenges. Her academic and professional work reflects her strong inclination towards designing better spatial quality for its user groups. She believes that architectural design of a space has its effects on the psychology of the user. Her research in the field of architectural psychology will help the user to design better for their well-being. She has graduated from Faculty of Architecture at Sri Sri University, Cuttack, Odisha, India, with a distinction and was conferred with Gold Medal for the Class of 2020.
In the Past, Surshree has completed her Internship at Sunil Patil and Associates in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Because of her sincere approach to the learning, she has received a scholarship from the office.
